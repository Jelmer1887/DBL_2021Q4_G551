A quick guide on how to use our visualisation tool

1. Uploading a .csv file

Simply click on the upload button on the navbar or on the "Upload your own csv" button! This can be done from any page.

2. The visualization page

The slider at the top of the page is used to select the start and end dates of the data you wish to analyze. You can also use the dropdown menus to choose 2 different visualizations, or use the 'fullscreen' buttons to view only one of them.

  • Force Directed Graph
  • Arc Diagram
  • Adjacency Matrix
  • Tree Map

3.1. Force Directed Graph

This visualization is a node-link diagram that in which the nodes are employees and the edges are emails. The emails can also be grouped togheter to limit clutter and the size of a node represents its throughput of emails.

4. Node Information Panel

To better understand the graph, you can click on a node to see more information about it, such as ID, emails sent and recieved or sentiment of emails.

5. Brush Select

If you want to follow a group of nodes throughout multiple visualizations, simply click on the "Brush Select" button and draw a box on the desired group.
